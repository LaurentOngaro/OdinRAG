# Daily - Template

> **How to use**:
>
> 1. Copy this file into `_Private/planning/daily/J_YYYY-MM-DD.md`
> 2. Rename the H1 title (`# Daily YYYY-MM-DD - <subject>`)
> 3. Fill in the date in the frontmatter
> 4. Check off as you go (`- [x]`)
> 5. At end of day, add a "Bilan" section at the bottom

```md

# Daily YYYY-MM-DD - Subject of the day

## Goal of the day

> One sentence that says **what must be delivered** by end of day. Not a list - a clear goal. If the goal is multiple, split into multiple dailies or reformulate.
> _Example: "Refactor `_Helpers/` with extraction of shared `lib/`"_

## Tasks

> Checklist. Fine granularity (1h max per task).

- [ ] First step
- [ ] Second step
- [ ] Step N

## Blockers / Questions

> Anything that blocks progress, or open question to discuss.

- _None for now_

## Decisions taken

> Technical decisions taken today (with short rationale).

- _None for now_

## Odin patterns / KB consulted

> Links to the KB files consulted today. Serves as history.

- _None for now_

## Tomorrow

> Top 1-3 things to do the next day.

1. _First thing_
2. _Second thing_

## Bilan (end of day)

> Filled **after** the session, not during. Short.

- **Delivered**: _what is actually done_
- **Postponed**: _what could not be done and why_
- **Mood note**: _(optional) general state_

```
